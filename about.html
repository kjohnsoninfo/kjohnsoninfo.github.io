About Me

Howdy! I'm Kara Johnson, a Software Engineer focused on building tools that bridge the gap between people and technology.

In my career, I've worked in various industries, including project engineering, healthcare technology, motion capture, and AAA (triple-A) game development. Drawing from this diverse background, I've used my versatile skill set to support multiple cross-disciplinary teams, such as data analysis, virtual production, cinematics, animation, and tools engineering. I love being able to create software that streamlines workflows, whether its ensuring the accuracy of clinical data pipelines or speeding up artist content creation. In all of my roles, I bring a deep passion for translating user needs into effective technical solutions and troubleshooting complex issues with others.

Want to chat? Feel free to email me!

I'm a Texas A&M graduate - Gig 'em! - and in my free time, you can find me playing sports, hiking in the mountains, or exploring new video games and books.
